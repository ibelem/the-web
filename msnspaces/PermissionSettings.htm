Permissions
 
Permissions specify who can view your space. You can choose to let everyone view your space or only people you know. Select one of the following, and then click Save.
Allows anyone on the Internet to view your space.
Allows only the people on your MSN Messenger Allow list to view your space. View my Allow list.
Allows only the people you select from your MSN Address Book to view your space.

Please note that any profile information that you enter is public even if your space permissions are set to Messenger or Private.
Select the check box beside each person who can view your space.
